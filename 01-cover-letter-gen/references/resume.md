# resume.md — Required Reference File

## What This Is
Your current CV or resume. The skill reads this to match your real experience against job requirements. Without it, the skill will ask you to paste your CV each time.

## What To Add
- Your full work history (job titles, organizations, dates, key responsibilities)
- Education (degrees, institutions, years)
- Publications, presentations, or academic work (if applicable)
- Skills, tools, certifications
- Awards or recognitions

## Format
Plain text or markdown. No need for fancy formatting — the skill reads the content, not the layout.

## Length
Include everything. More detail here = better letter customization.

## Example (first few lines)
```
Dr. Jane Smith
jane.smith@university.edu | (555) 123-4567

EDUCATION
PhD, Communication Studies, State University, 2019
Dissertation: "Solutions Journalism and Civic Engagement"

EXPERIENCE
Assistant Professor, Department of Journalism
State University | 2019–present
- Teach undergraduate and graduate courses in media studies and research methods
- Published 8 peer-reviewed articles in top-tier journals
```

# letter-template.md — Reference File

## What This Is
Your base cover letter template — the structural scaffolding and any boilerplate text you reuse across applications. The skill uses this to maintain your preferred format and any standard language you want preserved.

## What To Add
- Your preferred header format (name, contact info, date layout)
- Any standard opening or closing phrases you like
- Your preferred sign-off (e.g., "Warmest Regards," / "Sincerely,")
- Any language you want to keep consistent (e.g., a specific sentence about your research agenda)
- Notes on length preference (e.g., "always 1 page, max 450 words")

## Example
```
[Your Name]
[Email] | [Phone] | [Website/LinkedIn]
[Date]

[Hiring Committee / Hiring Manager Name]
[Department]
[Organization]

Dear [Title] [Last Name],

[Opening paragraph — tailored to role]

[Body paragraph 1 — strongest qualification]

[Body paragraph 2 — secondary qualifications]

[Closing — restate enthusiasm, forward-looking]

Warmest Regards,

[Your Name]
[Title, Institution]
```

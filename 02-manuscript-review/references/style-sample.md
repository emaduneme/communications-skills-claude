# style-sample.md — Reference File

## What This Is
A sample of your approved academic writing — 2–3 clean paragraphs that represent how you write at your best. The skill uses this to match your voice when flagging style issues and suggesting fixes during manuscript review.

## What To Add
Paste 2–3 paragraphs from a published paper, an accepted manuscript, or writing you consider polished and representative. Avoid drafts you're still working on — the goal is to capture your finished voice, not a work-in-progress.

## What "Style" Means Here
The skill looks at:
- Sentence length and structure (short and declarative vs. longer and complex)
- Hedging patterns ("suggests," "indicates," "implies" vs. stronger claims)
- Use of passive vs. active voice in methods and discussion
- How you introduce and define technical terms
- Transition patterns between paragraphs

## The Test

Before publishing anything, ask:

**1. Is it clear?** Could a smart person outside our organization understand this immediately?

**2. Is it honest?** Does every claim hold up? Are we owning our perspective, not hiding behind passive voice?

**3. Does it sound like us?** Read it aloud. If it sounds stiff, corporate, or generic — rewrite it.

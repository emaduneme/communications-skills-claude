# audit-worksheet.md — Brand Voice Discovery Worksheet

## What This Is
A structured worksheet to complete before building a brand voice guide. The skill uses your answers here to ground the voice attributes in your organization's actual mission, audience, and communication context — rather than generating generic adjectives.

## What To Add
Answer each section as specifically as possible. Vague answers produce vague voice guides.

## How To Use
Fill in this worksheet, then share it with the skill when prompted. You can paste it directly into the conversation or the skill will ask you these questions one by one.

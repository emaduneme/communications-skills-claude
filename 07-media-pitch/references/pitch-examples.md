# pitch-examples.md — Your Successful Pitch Archive

## What This Is
2-4 pitches you've sent that got a response — a reply, a story, a booking, or an op-ed acceptance. The skill uses these to match your pitching voice and understand what has worked for your specific context, credibility, and beat.

## What To Add
Paste real pitches you've sent. Include:
- The type of pitch (news story, op-ed, expert source, podcast)
- The outlet or journalist it was sent to (or just the publication type if you prefer privacy)
- Whether it resulted in coverage (helps calibrate what "worked" looks like for you)
- The pitch itself, as sent

Remove names if needed — just use [Reporter] or [Editor].

## Why This Matters
Generic pitches fail. The skill needs to understand your voice, your credibility signals, and the specific framing that lands for your expertise — not just follow a template.

## Example Structure
```

Outlet: Trade publication covering media industry
Result: Interview scheduled, story published

Subject: [paste subject line]

[paste pitch body]

Result: Accepted, published with edits
Sent: [approximate date]

[paste pitch]

---
```

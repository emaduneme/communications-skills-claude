# media-list-template.md — Your Media Contacts

## What This Is
A running list of journalists, editors, producers, and podcast hosts relevant to your beat. The skill uses this to suggest the right contact for a specific pitch and to personalize outreach based on what each person covers.

## What To Add
Add contacts as you build relationships. Include:
- Name and outlet
- Beat (what they specifically cover)
- Contact method (email preferred)
- Notes on relationship and past interactions
- Links to recent work (helps with personalization)

## Privacy Note
This file stays local — it is not shared anywhere. Add as much or as little detail as you're comfortable with.

## Example Structure
```
--- CONTACT 1 ---
Name: [First Last]
Outlet: [Publication name]
Title/Role: [Reporter / Senior Editor / Producer]
Beat: [Specific topics they cover — e.g., "AI and newsrooms, journalism education, media business"]
Email: [email if known]
Twitter/X: [@handle]
Notes: [Past interactions, relationship context, what kinds of pitches they've responded to]
Recent work: [Link to 1-2 recent articles]

---

--- CONTACT 2 ---
[Repeat structure]

---
```
